Simulation from a distribution
------------------------------
1. Suppose you wish to sample $ \theta_1, \theta_2 服从 p(\theta_1, \theta_2) $
2. You can only sample from 
    - $ p(\theta_1|\theta_2) $
    - $ p(\theta_2|\theta_1) $
3. Then you can use `Gibbs Distribution`.
